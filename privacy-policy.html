1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Greenfield Dental Clinic, operated through dentalimplantsinvietnam.com. We are a dental clinic based in Hanoi, Vietnam, providing dental implant services primarily to international patients including those from Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Canada.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: Hello@nhakhoagreenfield.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Dental condition or treatment interest
  • Message or enquiry details

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website or advertisement
  • Cookie identifiers (see Section 5)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your consultation request via your preferred contact method
  • To assess your suitability for dental implant treatment
  • To send you relevant information about our services
  • To improve our website and marketing campaigns
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising (Google Ads, Meta Ads)
  • To comply with legal obligations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for cookies and marketing tracking (you can withdraw at any time)
  • Legitimate interest — to respond to your enquiry and provide our services
  • Legal obligation — where required by applicable law

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and measure advertising effectiveness. We use Google Consent Mode v2, meaning tracking cookies are only activated after you give your consent.

Cookies we use

  • _ga, _ga_* — Google Analytics: identifies unique visitors and sessions (expires 2 years)
  • _gcl_au — Google Ads: links ad clicks to conversions (expires 90 days)
  • _fbp — Meta Pixel: identifies browsers for ad measurement (expires 90 days)

Managing your cookie preferences

You can accept or decline cookies using the banner displayed when you first visit our site. You can also clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Declining cookies will not affect your ability to submit a consultation request.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Tag Manager & Google Analytics — website analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Ads — advertising and conversion tracking
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — advertising and Lead event measurement
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — if you choose to contact us via WhatsApp

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Consultation enquiry data: up to 3 years
  • Patient treatment records: as required by Vietnamese medical law
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)

8. International Data Transfers

As a clinic based in Vietnam serving international patients, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your home country including Vietnam and the United States (Google and Meta servers). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for all such transfers.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent — for cookie-based tracking at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: Hello@nhakhoagreenfield.com

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. All data transmitted through our website is encrypted using SSL/TLS.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy
